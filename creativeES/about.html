ABOUT US

Creative Employment Services Inc. Is a Supportive Employment Community-Based Integration Training Program for intellectual disabled adult’s ages 18 years and older. Our area of focus will be to train and hire intellectual disability adults utilizing a person-centered approach that will cater to each individual unique need. Our goal is to provide ongoing support and training utilizing the person centered approach. We believe that through empowerment and inclusion we can help to enhance the quality of life of the individuals we serve.

STAFF EXPERIENCE

Creative Employment Services executive and management staff has over 15 years of experience supporting and providing an array of social and developmental services to intellectually disabled adults. We are experienced in assisting individuals with developmental disabilities with obtaining and maintaining employment opportunities. As well as, securing housing and teaching independent living skills. Our dedicated team will assist with providing a pathway to competitive integrated employment with community involvement. Creative Employment Services has working experience of Title 17, 22, Consumer’s Rights and certification in First -Aid, CPI, DSP I and DSP II.

STATEMENT OF PURPOSE

Creative Employment Services, Inc. is to support individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities through individualized services so that they are able to secure meaningful employment, gain independence and establish quality of life of their desires. Our main objective is to provide consumers with the opportunity to be involved with array of businesses and employers that promote a supportive employment environment within their community. The services we provide equip consumers with the essential job skills needed to become employable and successful individuals. We believe that through empowerment and inclusion Creative Employment Services can help to enhance the quality of life of each individual we serve by providing integrated and supportive employment within the community.
